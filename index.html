Panels Get Started

back
A panel must be a sibling to the header, content and footer elements inside a jQuery Mobile page.
You can add the panel markup either before or after these elements, but not in between.
A panel cannot be placed outside a page.
By default panel is closing after clicking outside of panel.
Oppen example panel
<div data-role="page" id="someID">
    <div data-role="header">Header content</div>
    <div data-role="main">
        Page content, and button to oppen panel.<br/>
        <a href="#panelID" class="ui-btn">Oppen example panel</a>
    </div>
    <div data-role="footer">footer content</div>
    <div data-role="panel" id="panelID">Here is some panel content.</div>
</div>
